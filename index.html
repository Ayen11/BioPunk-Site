About BioPunk

BioPunk is a story driven 2D psychological horror game which utilizes point and click elements to create and tell a compelling and immersive tale which includes puzzles, mini-games and compelling conversation with NPCs. The game explores the subject of abandonment and decline by highlighting a grandeur vs. loss dichotomy following a great war between nature and man.
